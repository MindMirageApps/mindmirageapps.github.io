Arithon Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Arithon is designed to operate locally on your device.

Information Collection

Arithon does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

The app does not require:

  • User accounts
  • Sign in
  • Cloud storage
  • Analytics tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location access
  • Camera access
  • Contacts access

Local Device Storage

Arithon stores certain app settings locally on your device using on-device storage. This may include:

  • Calculator mode preferences
  • Saved setup values
  • Intro/tutorial completion status

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Mind Mirage Apps or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Arithon does not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

Data Sharing

No personal data is collected or shared.

Security

Because Arithon operates locally and does not transmit user data, no personal information is stored on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Arithon is not directed toward children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
