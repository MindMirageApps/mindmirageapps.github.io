Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Mind Mirage Apps respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across our mobile apps and website.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app, we may collect limited information needed to provide app functionality, such as account details, app progress, preferences, purchases, and basic usage data.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve our apps
  • Save user progress and preferences
  • Support purchases or subscriptions
  • Maintain app security and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services such as analytics, authentication, advertising, payment processing, cloud storage, and crash reporting. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

Some apps may display ads. Where advertising is used, it may be provided by third-party ad networks. We aim to configure advertising responsibly and in line with applicable platform requirements.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are handled through the relevant app store provider. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent is required. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@mindmirageapps.com
