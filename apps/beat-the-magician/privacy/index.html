Beat The Magician Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Beat The Magician is designed for local gameplay and does not collect personal data in version 1.

Information Collection

Beat The Magician does not require an account and does not collect account data, names, email addresses, passwords, or other personal information.

The app currently does not use:

  • Firebase or cloud databases
  • Cloud sync
  • Advertising services
  • Tracking technologies
  • Subscriptions
  • Multiplayer services

Local Device Storage

Beat The Magician stores gameplay stats and settings locally on your device only. This local data may include:

  • Wins and losses
  • Streaks
  • Daily challenge status
  • Difficulty preference
  • Sound preference
  • Haptics preference

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Mind Mirage Apps or any third party.

Resetting Stats

You can reset gameplay stats inside the app. Resetting stats removes the locally stored gameplay progress controlled by that reset option on your device.

Advertising and Tracking

Beat The Magician does not currently include advertising, ad identifiers, analytics tracking, or third-party tracking services.

Cloud Sync and Accounts

Beat The Magician does not currently offer cloud sync and does not require users to create or sign in to an account.

Future Services

Future updates may add optional services, such as cloud features, analytics, ads, purchases, or other app functionality. If optional services are added, this Privacy Policy may be updated to explain what data is involved and how it is used.

Children's Privacy

Beat The Magician is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
