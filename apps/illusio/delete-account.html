Delete Your Illusio Account

You can delete your Illusio account directly inside the app at any time:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

What is deleted

When you delete your account, the following linked account data is removed:

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Cloud progression data
  • XP/Tokens linked to the account
  • Completed lesson progress linked to the account

Local guest data

If you used Illusio as a guest, some local progress data may remain on your device unless you delete the app or clear local app data.

Need help?

For support with account deletion, contact support@mindmirageapps.com.
