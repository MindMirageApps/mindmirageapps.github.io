Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Mind Mirage Apps respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across our mobile apps and website.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app, we may collect limited information required to provide functionality, such as account data, app progress, preferences, purchases, and basic usage data.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve our apps
  • Save user progress and preferences
  • Support purchases or subscriptions
  • Maintain security and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services such as analytics, authentication, advertising, payments, cloud storage, and crash reporting. These services may process data under their own policies.

Advertising

Some apps may display ads. Where used, these are provided by third-party ad networks configured in line with platform requirements.

Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by the app store provider. We do not store payment card details.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children where consent is required.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and maintain system integrity.
