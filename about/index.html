About Mind Mirage Apps

Mind Mirage Apps is an independent app studio that creates and publishes its own mobile products. The portfolio focuses on magic learning, relationship play, focused utilities, and atmospheric game concepts.

What we make

The studio builds apps with clear purposes: Illusio helps people practice magic through structured lessons, Couple Questions & Games supports playful conversations for two, and Arithon Calculator offers a precise everyday utility. Upcoming concepts are presented transparently as they develop.

How we work

Every product is shaped around a focused use case, a polished dark interface, readable copy, and straightforward availability information. The goal is simple: make mobile apps that feel intentional, useful, and easy to trust.
