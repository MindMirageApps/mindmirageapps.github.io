Magic Atlas Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Magic Atlas (“the App”) is operated by Mind Mirage Apps LTD. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication information
  • Basic profile information

App Usage Data

We may store:

  • Lesson progress
  • Completed techniques and tricks
  • Streak information
  • Credit balances
  • App settings and preferences

Device & Technical Data

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Sync progress across devices
  • Maintain streaks and lesson progress
  • Process purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Prevent abuse and unauthorized access

Firebase & Third-Party Services

Magic Atlas uses third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Apple services
  • Google AdMob (if applicable)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Advertising

Magic Atlas may display advertisements. Current versions use non-personalized advertising where applicable.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card information.

Data Retention

We retain account and progress data while your account remains active or as necessary to provide app functionality.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Stop using the app at any time

Requests can be made by contacting: support@mindmirageapps.com

Children’s Privacy

Magic Atlas is not directed toward children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
