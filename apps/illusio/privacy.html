Illusio Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Illusio is a Mind Mirage Apps learning experience. This Privacy Policy explains how data is used when you access Illusio features.

Information we collect

  • Firebase Authentication: Illusio uses Firebase Authentication to sign users in and manage account access.
  • Firestore progression data: Illusio stores progression-related learning data in Firestore so progress can sync across sessions and devices.
  • Local device storage: Illusio may save settings and temporary app state on your device for a faster and smoother experience.

Optional rewarded ads

Illusio may add optional rewarded ads in the future. If enabled, ad providers may process limited technical data needed to serve and measure ads in line with their policies.

How data is used

Data is used to operate the app, support sign-in, preserve progression, improve reliability, and provide support when requested.

Account deletion support

You can request account deletion support at any time. We will help process account and related data deletion requests in accordance with applicable requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion support, contact: support@mindmirageapps.com.
